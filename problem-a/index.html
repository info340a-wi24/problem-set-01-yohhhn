Captain Holt

Raymond Holt, played by Andre Braugher, is the captain of the fictional 99th precinct in the show Brooklyn 99. As a gay, black police officer, he has faced much discrimination, shaping much of his no-nonsense attitude and strict adherence to rules. However, throughout the show he learns to act less like a robot thanks to his squad.

  • His honesty and accountability is what makes him an admirable mentor and leader
  • While he takes his work (very very) seriously, he learns to how to have fun
  • He cares deeply for the people around him and is willing to put his career and life on the line for them
